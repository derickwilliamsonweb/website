My Story

The Unveiling of Derrick Williamson's Personal Odyssey

Derrick Williamson's extensive background in ministry and community work led to a close relationship with the Hardees, a family deeply involved in volunteer work and generous contributions to various causes. Over time, their shared dedication to making a difference in the lives of others fostered a familial dynamic. Derrick opened his home to Janet, Jordan, and Claire during a crisis involving Jordan's pregnancy, solidifying their bond as a chosen family.

In a surprising turn of events, Janet Hardee expressed a desire to gift a valuable farm property in Morgan County, Georgia, to Derrick's ministry. Initially skeptical due to the property's high value, Derrick eventually agreed to accept the generous gift. The deed was intended to be in the ministry's name, a decision made to ensure clarity and permanence, affirming the property as an unadulterated gift as per Janet's intentions.

The relationship between Derrick and the Hardees took a negative turn when the value of the gifted property increased. The Hardees, for reasons not entirely clear, attempted to sell the property against Derrick's wishes. This marked the beginning of legal battles, false reports, and a series of actions aimed at dismantling Derrick's ownership of the property and seizing control.

Derrick's attempts to seek legal recourse, including a restraining order, were met with hurdles as the legal system presented limitations on eligibility. The Hardees, assisted by Judge Holt, escalated their efforts by making false reports to government agencies, leading to investigations and legal complexities. The theft of animal exhibit panels and company assets further intensified the injustice faced by Derrick and his animal sanctuary.

As Derrick delved deeper into the legal battle, evidence emerged of a corrupt alliance involving Judge Holt and those conspiring against him. This revelation shed light on a network extending beyond immediate adversaries, exposing a tangled web of deceit. Tragedy struck with the loss of Derrick's brother, heightening the personal and legal stakes in his fight for justice against a corrupt alliance aiming to seize his family's farm.
